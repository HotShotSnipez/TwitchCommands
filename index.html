Twitch Commands

!rank

Shows which rank I am in Valorant.

!crosshair

Shows the crosshair I'm using in Valorant.

!discord

Gives a link to my Discord server.

!lurk

Tells me that you are lurking in the stream.

!followage

Displays the next song in the queue.

!watchtime

Displays the next song in the queue.

!pc

Shows my PC specs.

!setup

Shows the gear I use for my setup.

!socials

Gives a link to all my socials via Linktree.

!song

Tells which song we are listening to.

!link

Provides a link to the current song.

!queue

lets you queue songs (!queue artist name song name)

!nextqueue

Displays the next song in the queue.
